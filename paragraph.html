Paragraph Samples





A well-organized paragraph supports or develops a single controlling idea,which is expresed in a sentence called the topic sentence.A topic sentence has several important functions:it substantiates or supports an essay's thesis statement;





Although most Paragraph should have a topic sentence, there are a few situations when a Paragraph might not need a topic sentence.For example,you might be able to omit a topic sentence in a Paragraph that narrates a series of events.
